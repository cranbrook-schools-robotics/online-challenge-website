Cranbrook hosted robotics camps
Robotics Day Camps utilize applied robotics to explore the realms of science, technology, engineering, and math. Working with LEGO® and VEX™ IQ software packages, campers design, build, and program autonomous and remote-controlled robots to experience challenges and participate in competitions.
