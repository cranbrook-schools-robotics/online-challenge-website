Bot Description:

A DR4B capable of stacking up to 11 cones, and scoring in both the 20 and 10 point zones. The robot uses a gyro, IMEs and other sensors to run consistent high value autonomous routines.

Our team has existed for 5 years now, we have gone to worlds 4 times and ranked 8th at the US Open in 2014. We consistently rank among the top teams in Michigan.
