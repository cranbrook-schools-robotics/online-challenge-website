Cranbrook Robotics Organization

Welcome to Cranbrook Robotics!

We are a robotics program from Michigan. Currently we are the biggest robotics program in Michigan, with around 20 teams in both VEX, VEX IQ, and FTC. This is Our 19th year of doing Robotics, and as a club we have participated in the very first VEX tournament. Our Club provides students from elementary school to high school the opportunity to compete in robotics and pursue a career in math, science, and engineering. While doing this, our outreach of the club has helped many other robotics teams in Michigan start-up.
