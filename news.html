Impressive Competition at the Cranbrook VRC Tournament

1/3/2018

On December 16th, Cranbrook hosted a VRC tournament. Cranbrook teams also participated in this tournament, and these teams performed very well. Cranbrook teams received two regional qualifications, along with other awards. The event was very competitive and all of the teams that attended were incredibly impressive.

November 11th was an exciting day for the VexIQ students: the competition at Waterford. Two Cranbrook teams won the championship, along with incredible domination in the skills listings. Congratulations to all of the Cranbrook teams that participated in these events!

As the day began, it became clear to everyone present that Cranbrook would be a force to be reckoned with. After much fun and riveting competition, two Cranbrook teams ended up winning the competition along with many other honors being awarded to CK teams (see below for complete list.) Thank you all for coming and congratulations CK!
